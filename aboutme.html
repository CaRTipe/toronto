THREADS OF TORONTO

In Nakuru's heart, where the streets pulse with
energy and sunsets paint the sky gold, lives a boy who speaks through
style. Toronto doesn't just wear clothes—he tells stories. Each
outfit is a feeling. A moment. A mood. One day calm in crisp whites,
the next loud in vintage fire. He hunts for pieces others miss and
brings them to life—because fashion, to him, isn't about being seen.
It's about being understood. He doesn't follow trends. He curves them.
And even in silence, the world notices.
